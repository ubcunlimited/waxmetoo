# Wax Me Too — Design Brainstorm

## Design Philosophy Options

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Editorial Boutique — inspired by modern beauty editorial magazines (Vogue Beauty, Byrdie, Into The Gloss)

**Core Principles:**
1. Asymmetric editorial layouts with intentional text-image tension
2. Generous whitespace as a luxury signal — content breathes
3. Typography-forward sections that carry emotional weight without imagery
4. Conversion clarity: every section has a single clear action

**Color Philosophy:** Warm ivory (#F7F3EE) as the dominant canvas — the site should feel like a beautifully printed lookbook. Deep espresso (#3B2F2A) anchors all type. Muted rose nude (#CFA7A0) is used sparingly as an accent — a lip stain, not a blush. Sage (#A8B3AA) signals calm and eco-credibility. Champagne beige (#D8C6B6) for card fills and hover states.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset grid — hero sections use split layouts with text left and image right (or reversed). Service cards use a masonry-style stagger. Sections break the horizontal rhythm with diagonal or angled transitions.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin circular line-art motifs (echoing the logo's circular badge) used as section dividers and background accents
- Oversized serif display type for section headings (Cormorant Garamond)
- Hairline borders and subtle dot/line patterns as texture

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal warmth — cards lift gently with a champagne shadow. CTAs use a fill-sweep animation on hover. Images scale subtly on hover.

**Animation:** Fade-up entrance animations (staggered 80ms per element). Smooth scroll-linked parallax on hero image. Button hover: background fills from left with rose nude color.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (600 italic) — for hero headlines and section titles
- Subheadings: Cormorant Garamond (400 regular) — for service names
- Body: DM Sans (400/500) — clean, modern, readable
- UI/Labels: DM Sans (600 uppercase, tracked) — for nav, buttons, tags

<idea>
**Design Movement:** Modern Feminine Craft — the visual language of high-end beauty brands like Tatcha, Saie, and Nécessaire meets a boutique spa

**Core Principles:**
1. Layered depth — backgrounds, cards, and overlays create a sense of physical space
2. Warm photography integration — images feel curated, not stock
3. Confident femininity — curves, warmth, and softness without being delicate
4. Conversion architecture embedded into the design system

**Color Philosophy:** Ivory (#F7F3EE) as the base. Espresso (#3B2F2A) for all structural type. Rose nude (#CFA7A0) as the brand accent — used for CTAs, active states, and brand moments. Champagne (#D8C6B6) for card backgrounds and subtle section fills. Sage (#A8B3AA) for wellness/eco callouts. The palette should feel like a well-appointed treatment room.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric two-column layouts for hero and feature sections. Service cards in a 3-column grid on desktop, single column on mobile. Location cards use a horizontal scroll on mobile. Full-bleed image sections with text overlays for emotional impact.

**Signature Elements:**
- Soft organic shape dividers between sections (gentle wave or arc SVG)
- Circular image crops echoing the logo badge
- Rose nude underline/highlight treatment on key phrases

**Interaction Philosophy:** Tactile and responsive — cards have a subtle lift shadow on hover. CTAs pulse gently on load. Mobile sticky booking button appears after scrolling past the hero.

**Animation:** Scroll-triggered fade-up for cards and content blocks. Hero text animates in with a stagger. Smooth page transitions. Booking button slides up from bottom on mobile.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond (600 italic) — for hero and emotional moments
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond (500) — section titles
- Body: DM Sans (400) — clean and approachable
- UI: DM Sans (600, uppercase, tracked) — nav, buttons, labels

## Selected Design: Modern Feminine Craft (Option 3)

**Rationale:** This approach best balances the brand's dual identity — boutique beauty with mass-market accessibility. The layered depth creates a premium feel without being intimidating. The conversion architecture is baked into the design system, supporting the primary goal of increasing bookings. The typography pairing of Cormorant Garamond + DM Sans is distinctive, warm, and modern.
